Accommodation

We have Luxury Chalets, Comfortable Guestrooms and Family Units available.

Activities

All activities take place on site, with magical views and exceptional service. Choose from Sand-boarding, Horse-riding, Fat-Biking, Picnics, hiking and more.

Restaurant

Our fully licensed restaurant and bar offers appetizing meals and is ideal for celebrating special occasions... With great views of the ocean, dunes and swimming pool.

Weddings & Functions

Oyster Bay Lodge is the perfect destination for intimate functions. We specialize in small private weddings on a private, desolated beachfront using horses as a beautiful backdrop

Welcome to Oyster Bay Lodge

We are a 4-Star Luxury lodge, situated on a 235 hectare natural coastal reserve. We have 4 "Cape Dutch" chalets and 10 comfortable guestrooms to choose from, as well as a host of exciting activities.
