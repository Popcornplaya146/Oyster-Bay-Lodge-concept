On-site Activities

Travel along the dunes in a dune safari vehicle with a dedicated guide who will tell you all about the reserve and the natural surroundings. Birding, whale watching, sun-downers and night drives, are just some of the few highlights to experience on our Dune Safari. Maximum 10 guests, 5 per vehicle.

R400 pp

4 different hiking trails have been set out on the Coastal reserve. All the hiking trails are marked with different colors and vary from 3 to 7,5 kilometers. Breath in the fresh air of the “fynbos” and listen to the sounds of nature and extensive bird llife
Distance per Trail:
Green Trail 7.5 kilometer
Blue Trail 6.5 kilometer
Red Trail 5 kilometer
Yellow Trail 3 kilometer

R30 pp

Enjoy an unforgettable experience while exploring the Coastal Reserve on horseback and have a gallop or trot along the 3,5 kilometer private beach next to the waves of the Indian Ocean. This activity always goes out with an experienced guide/s and we have various horses, which are even suitable for the “first-timers” or children..

R400 pp

To look after your appetite, order a picnic on one of the 4 viewing decks. The friendly staff will gladly prepare a delicious picnic basket for you. Just let us now when and where and one of the experienced guides will bring the basket to you on horseback!
There is also a Luxury Dune Picnic available. Please inquire about rates and what it includes.

R165 pp

Enjoy a South African braai in our rustic Boma. With your private chef this is the perfect setting to have a night out or a family gathering. Enjoy a traditional 3 course dinner, which includes a starter, a main and a lovely desert. At the end of your evening enjoy a relaxed stroll back to the lodge, with beautiful views of the Milky Way. Bookings need to be placed before 15:00. Tours depart at 17:30. (Minimum of 2 persons).

R500 pp

Spoil yourself with a relaxing massage by hiring a massage therapist. Massages take place at the wellness center, the Boma. Please choose your preferred therapy from below and lets help you relax and unwind. Back and Neck massage or a Full Body Massage.

R500.00 - R600.00 pp

Explore our property with our guide. Meet us at the bar for an introduction to fat biking an safety precautions. All tours go to the beach and into the dunes (if you can make it). On this tour you will be surrounded bu the natural Fynbos and their aromas. The terrain is ever changing, from grassy stretches, sandy spots steep descents or accents. The tour lasts 1:30 min.

R500 pp

Have you ever pictured yourself standing on the top of a dune and see yourself sliding down on a sand board. Feel the wind blow through your hair, while there is nothing but serine beauty surrounding you. You will be shown the ropes at the lodge and you can go at your own pace with the sand board. 3 hours of usage.

R300.00 per a board
